About Our School

Our History

Established in 1965, High School Udhwa has been a beacon of quality education in the Sahibganj district for over 60 years. What started as a small secondary school has grown into a prestigious +2 institution offering both Arts and Science streams.

Over the decades, we have produced countless successful professionals including doctors, engineers, civil servants, and educators who are making significant contributions to society.

Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement, adapting to modern educational needs while maintaining traditional values of discipline and academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and moral values in students, preparing them to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence that empowers students from all backgrounds to achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to community development.
